Animation

Throughout my career, I have used animation to elevate storytelling and player engagement - bringing characters, cutscenes, and visual effects to life, and enhancing both immersion and gameplay responsiveness.

Experience

  • Created 2D character animations (walk, run, attack) using Aseprite and imported them into Unity for sprite-based games.
  • Developed 3D animations in Blender, including rigging and animating a stylized boss character for a dungeon crawler.
  • Designed cutscene animations using Unity Timeline, synchronized with music and voiceover for immersive storytelling.
  • Implemented smooth UI animations like button bounce, fade-ins, and scene transitions to enhance game flow.
  • Collaborated with designers to animate environmental elements like moving platforms, doors, and destructible objects in Unreal Engine.
  • Applied squash and stretch principles to enemy animations, increasing visual clarity and player satisfaction during combat.
