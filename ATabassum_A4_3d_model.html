3D Modeling

I have applied 3D modeling extensively throughout my career to build immersive game environments and characters-ranging from optimized assets for mobile to high-detail models for cinematic scenes as 3D modeling serves as the visual backbone of gameplay experiences.

Experience

  • Created environment props and character models using Blender and 3DS Max for multiple indie game projects.
  • Sculpted high-resolution models in Blender and ZBrush, later retopologized for performance optimization in games.
  • Textured 3D assets using Substance Painter, applying physically based rendering (PBR) workflows for realism.
  • Rigged and animated humanoid and creature models using Blender's Rigify and Mixamo pipelines.
  • Worked closely with Unity and Unreal Engine developers to ensure seamless model integration with proper LODs and collision meshes.
  • Optimized asset performance by reducing poly count and baking details from high-poly to low-poly models.
  • Participated in weekly sprint meetings, showcasing 3D progress and collaborating with designers and VFX artists.
  • Adapted assets for VR and AR platforms with attention to user immersion, scale accuracy, and performance metrics.
